The Vision

Imagine a web of lectures, videos, interactive content, and more on hundreds of topics. Each skill you learn linked to higher skills. But more than just building up, but a system that can help you find holes in your knowledge, and direct you to content that can help you fill those gaps. Imagine a system that could keep track of what skills you'd learning, could reccomend topics for further learning or even suggest entirely new paths.

Finally, imagine this as not a small collection of videos or lectures, but a world wide source for learning. Allowing anyone to add content, link educational videos they found, or anything else they found. Instructions from thousands of sources on anything you could want to learn. From Math to CS, Art, History, and How to maintain your car.
